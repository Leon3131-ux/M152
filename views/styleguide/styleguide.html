About Styleguide

We use style guides to give our UI or website personality, that reflects our corporate design.
Different colors have different meanings and trigger different kindd of emotional reactions.

For our website we’ve decided to go a rather dark style, we don’t have a particular reason for it, other that we just like the color scheme.

We also took the freedom to add brighter indicator color for success messages or warning messages.

For the font we decided to take “Inconsolata”, this font is commonly used in website that software developers use and develop, and we also like it
